2017 FRC Steamworks Competition

Game Challenges

The 2017 competition was themed around steampunk flying ships and featured multiple objectives to score points. The robots could shoot plastic balls into either a low goal or a high goal. The high goal was harder to get the balls into, but granted 3x the points per ball. Teams were also granted bonus points if they could get a certain number of balls into the goals. Additonally, the robots could move gears across the field in order to get a maximum of four rotors on a mock airship to spin. Each rotor required an increasing number of gears to get started, and the teams earned bonus points if all four rotors were spinning. At the end of the match, ropes would decend from the airships, and teams would gain points for each robot that could climb it to a certain height. For more info, watch the video to the right
